# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on solving the problem.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, and exclusionary behavior.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
